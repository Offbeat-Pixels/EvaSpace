How We Make
Work Better for Everyone

At EvaSpaces, we design inclusive, RPWD-compliant workspaces and interiors that are both functional and inspiring. Specializing in disability-friendly construction, accessible coworking, and barrier-free design, we empower every individual to work, connect, and thrive.

We graduated to creating office spaces from hosting homes on Airbnb. Hosting has been an incredibly rewarding experience for us. We carry the same ethos to our office space business. Whether you're welcoming guests into your home for a stay or clients into your workspaces . At its core, great hosting is about creating a welcoming atmosphere, making people feel comfortable, and ensuring that the workspaces adapts to the need of its users. In a world full of commercial overtures , a warm and personalised touch goes a long way in making your clients feel truly special.

We both ensure that each & every item in our office spaces is handpicked. We would like to give an experience to our clients, so that they love coming back to work !
