What Sets Us Apart

Open To Curation

The office spaces have been designed in a manner that the soft partitions can be remodified to align with the design proposed by the company. In addition to that the open spaces on one floor can be converted into a small office for early-stage businesses.

Centrally Located

Evaspaces is located in the IT Park in Dehradun which is fast growing to be the best commercial hub not just in the city but in the state also.
IT Park is now bustling with companies which have moved from the NCR and other cities. It is well connected from all the parts of the city and has some of the best places to stay within 5kms of it.

Experiential Integrated Workspace

At Evaspaces, we understand that in today’s times efficiency at work is not just restricted to a beautifully crafted workspaces but also spills over to ancillary requirements of a breakout space, a place to have satiate the food cravings and the mid-meal hunger, and a hobnobbing space for office get togethers and hence we have focused on creating a space, where all these are integrated into one.

Guest Retreat

Every client of ours has the option of booking the space for their guests, transiting employees or new joinees.
Not restricted for in-bound customers, the BnB can also be booked for work ‘stay-backs’ of your employees, who will always have the option of retiring in the bnb, and not take the long ride back home.

Tailored Dine-In Options

A full-fledged café and a well-equipped kitchen provide our clients with an option to offer tailored meal packages to their employees along with the different delectable cuisines that are served at the café ranging from home cooked meal, mouth-watering snacks, healthy meal options to a full course thali.

Rooftop Hobnobbing Space

As part of experiential workspaces, we have a full-scale event space where you can conduct your employee engagement activities, celebrate company achievements, have those quick working lunches or informal get togethers.
